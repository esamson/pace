pace

Keeps an eye on long-running commands for you. Notifies you via Pushbullet.

Download as .zip Download as .tar.gz View on GitHub

Just add pace to your $PATH. The first time pace is run, you will be given instructions for authorizing it to push notifications to your Pushbullet account. Once authorized, just prepend any command with pace:

$ pace make

This executes make and sends a Pushbullet notification once execution finishes.
